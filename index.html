FAQ

Common Questions

Quick answers to what our guests ask most.

What are you known for? 🍛
We're best known for our Butter Chicken, Chicken Tikka Masala, Tandoori Chicken, Paneer Tikka Masala, Garlic Naan, Chicken Biryani, Lamb Curry, and Vegetable Samosa.
What meals do you serve? 🕐
We serve Lunch and Dinner. Lunch hours are 11am – 3pm and Dinner hours are 4:30pm – 9pm (9:30pm on Fri & Sat).
Do you offer delivery or takeout? 🚚
Yes! We offer both takeout and delivery. You can place your order online at our ordering portal or call us directly at (925) 485-4800.
Do you offer catering? 🎉
Absolutely. We cater for weddings, corporate events, birthdays, religious ceremonies, and all special occasions. Use the inquiry form above or visit our catering portal.
Do you have vegetarian options? 🥗
Yes, we have a wide selection of vegetarian dishes including Paneer Tikka Masala, Palak Paneer, Dal Makhani, Vegetable Biryani, Samosa, and many more.
Where are you located? 📍
We are located at 210 Rose Ave, Pleasanton, CA 94566. Free parking is available on-site. We're easy to find right in the heart of Pleasanton.
